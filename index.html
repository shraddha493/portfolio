What the Numbers Show

RAG performs best when answers live directly in documents. Complexity and ambiguity reduce performance — a predictable and instructive finding for production deployment.

Factual (4.3/5) — Clear retrieval, accurate grounding
Synthesis (3.9/5) — Multi-doc retrieval occasionally misses a chunk
Edge-Case (3.9/5) — Confident hallucination is the main failure mode
Fix: stronger uncertainty signals + multi-hop retrieval
